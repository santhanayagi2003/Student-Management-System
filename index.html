Student Management System

This system is designed to simplify and organize academic information within a college environment. It helps manage important student-related activities such as maintaining student records, managing teacher information, organizing courses, tracking attendance, monitoring grades, and handling enrollments. It provides a structured platform where all academic data can be accessed and updated easily. By bringing all essential information together in one place, the system helps improve efficiency, accuracy, and overall management of college operations.
